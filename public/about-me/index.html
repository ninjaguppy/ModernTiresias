About

I am a first year PhD student at Washington University in Saint Louis. During my undergraduate degree I had lots of space to write. While I love spending my days thinking about sheaves, analytic functions, and cohomology, I wanted a place to get the thoughts out of my head and onto a page.

If all goes well, there should be a post here every Sunday on whatever thoughts have wedged themselves into the caffeine-addled brain of a graduate student preparing for qualifying exams. I tend to think about memory, narrative, beauty, among other things, but the last thing I want to do is put my work here in a box. I make no promises about the quality of the posts here—this is a space primarily for me to process my own thoughts—but if you’re interested in what I have to say, please follow along!
